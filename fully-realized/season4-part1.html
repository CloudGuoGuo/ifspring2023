Season 4 Part 1

Season 4 Part 1 of Attack on Titan takes place several years after the events of Season 3, with a new protagonist, Gabi Braun, a young girl from the nation of Marley, which is at war with Paradis Island, the setting of the previous seasons.

The season focuses on the conflict between Marley and Paradis Island, as Gabi and other Marleyan warriors infiltrate the island to capture the "Founding Titan" and "Attack Titan". Along the way, they encounter Eren and his friends, who are now fighting for the freedom of their people within the walls.

The season also explores the backstory of some of the key characters, including the history of the world and the origins of the Titans. It delves into the political and ideological conflicts between Marley and Paradis Island, and raises questions about the nature of power, freedom, and justice.

As the season progresses, the conflicts between the characters deepen, leading to intense and emotional moments as they grapple with their own beliefs and loyalties. The season ends with a cliffhanger, as Eren and his friends launch a daring attack on Marley, promising a thrilling and epic continuation in the final season.

Overall, Season 4 Part 1 of Attack on Titan is a complex and thought-provoking continuation of the series, exploring new characters and conflicts while also building upon the themes and mysteries established in the previous seasons. It sets up the story for an epic and emotional conclusion in the final season, leaving the audience eager for more.
