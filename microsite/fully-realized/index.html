Attack on Titan is a popular Japanese anime TV series that belongs to the action, drama, and dark fantasy genres. The series began airing on April 7, 2013, and the final season released on March 28, 2021. The last part of the final season will be released on fall 2023. There are a total of four seasons in the Attack on Titan TV series, with 75 episodes in total. The first three seasons consist of 59 episodes and the final season is divided into two parts with 16 episodes in total. The show is based on the manga series of the same name written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. The anime was produced by Wit Studio and MAPPA, and directed by Tetsuro Araki, Masashi Koizuka, and Jun Shishido. The show's main writers include Yasuko Kobayashi, Hiroshi Seko, and Ryo Suzukaze. Attack on Titan can be watched on various streaming platforms such as Netflix, Hulu, and Crunchyroll.
