Season 2

Season 2 of Attack on Titan continues the story of Eren Yeager and his friends as they try to uncover the secrets of the Titans and protect humanity from their threat. The season focuses on the mystery surrounding the Titans and the internal conflicts within the walls that protect humanity.

The season opens with the discovery of a new type of Titan that is smaller and more agile than the others. This "Beast Titan" seems to possess intelligence and is able to communicate with humans. Meanwhile, Eren begins to have strange dreams about a mysterious girl who may hold the key to the Titans' origins.

As the season progresses, Eren and his friends face new challenges and enemies, including the revelation of a secret group within the walls that seeks to manipulate the Titans for their own purposes. They also encounter a group of soldiers from an isolated community outside the walls who have their own agenda and secrets.

The season culminates in a battle against the Titans in the forest, where Eren and his friends face off against the Beast Titan and the revelation of the true identity of the Titan Shifters. The season ends with a cliffhanger as the characters are left reeling from the revelations and unsure of what to do next.

Overall, Season 2 of Attack on Titan delves deeper into the mysteries of the Titans and expands the world and characters of the series, while also delivering intense action and emotional moments that keep the audience invested in the story
