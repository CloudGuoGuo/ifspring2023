Season 3-part1

Season 3 Part 1 of Attack on Titan begins with the aftermath of the battle against the Titans in the forest. Eren and his friends are now tasked with investigating the secrets of the walls that protect humanity, leading them to uncover a conspiracy within the government that threatens the very survival of humanity.

As they dig deeper, they discover the truth about the origin of the Titans and the history of their world, which is far more complex and horrifying than they could have ever imagined. They also encounter new enemies, including the mysterious "Beast Titan" and a group of Titan Shifters who are after Eren's power.

The season also explores the backstories of some of the key characters, including Levi and Historia, revealing their traumatic pasts and how they became soldiers in the fight against the Titans. Along the way, they face moral dilemmas and conflicts as they struggle to reconcile their duty to humanity with their own personal desires and beliefs.

As the season progresses, the conspiracy within the government is exposed, and Eren and his friends find themselves caught up in a struggle for power that threatens to tear apart the very fabric of society. The season ends with a shocking revelation that changes everything, leaving the characters and the audience uncertain of what is to come.

Overall, Season 3 Part 1 of Attack on Titan is a thrilling and complex continuation of the story, delving deeper into the world and characters of the series while also delivering intense action and emotional moments that keep the audience engaged and invested in the story.
